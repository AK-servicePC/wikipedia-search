How it works

You can search Wikipedia straight from the 'W' button in the toolbar. Just click it, type the article you want, and press Enter. The results will open in a new tab.

Wikipedia Classic 5.0 is a fork of Wikipedia Search 4.1.1, for people who want to keep the older button search instead of the new integration with Chrome's search bar and right-click functionality. Wikipedia Search Classic also brings bug fixes and new languages from the newer updates.

Wikipedia Search Classic, just like Wikipedia Search, is open-source under the GPLv3 license on GitHub. Please note that Classic is a seperate branch from the newer Wikipedia Search, but still on the same repository.
