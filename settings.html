Getting Started

To search, just type 'wiki' in the address bar, then a space, then the name of the article you want. Live suggestions from Wikipedia are displayed as you type, in your selected language. In addition, you can select any word or phrase on the web, right-click, and search it in Wikipedia instantly.

Wikipedia Search will try to auto-detect your preferred language, but you can change it manually by clicking the 'Change language' button above.
