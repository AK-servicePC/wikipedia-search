Welcome to Wikipedia Search 5!

New ways to search

You may notice that the old W button is missing. That's because you can now search straight from the search bar!

Just type 'wiki', then a space, then the name of the article you want. Live suggestions from Wikipedia are automatically displayed as you type, and in the language you pick!

In addition, you can now right-click on any word or phrase on the web and search it in Wikipedia instantly.
